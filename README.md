# Introducing ROCKY!
## Free & easy OKR tracking for companies & teams.


Track OKRS across multiple teams so you can keep an eye on your entire portfolio of activity. Track narrative, confidence, and next actions across your entire portfolio of teams to instantly see how everyone is doing, where people need help and ensure teams are having valuable conversations around OKRs to steer themselves towards success.

Rocky is an OKR tracking tool designed and built by Peter Kappus using 20+ years of experience helping companies to define, organise, and deliver outstanding results along with over 4 years of direct OKR coaching and training experience. Learn more.
